Game Rules

. The game has 20 questions about different cultural and geographical topics
. You have 10 seconds to answer each question, and press the "Check Answer" button
. You only score when you press the "Check Answer" button
. You have just one attempt!
. There are three levels of questions:
-Easy gives you 1 points
-Medium gives you 2 points
-Difficult gives you 3 points
